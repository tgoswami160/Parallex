Winter

Winter is the coldest season, marked by short days, long nights, and often snow and ice in many regions.

Summer

Summer is the warmest season, characterized by long days, short nights, and abundant sunshine.

Spring

Spring is the season of renewal, marked by blooming flowers, warmer days, and new growth after winter.

Autumn

Autumn is the season of transition, marked by cooler days, falling leaves, and vibrant hues of red, orange, and gold.
